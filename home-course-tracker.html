This is your course-specific handicap index — what your handicap would be if only rounds from this course were submitted to GHIN. Calculated using the full USGA scoring differential formula: (Score − Course Rating) × 113 ÷ Slope. The best differentials are averaged per USGA guidelines (best 1 of 3, best 1 of 4, best lowest 2 of 5+, etc.).
